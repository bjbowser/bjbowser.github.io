How Did I Get Where I Am A Quick Intro

Beth Bowser has been an avid reader with a passion for writing since she was a girl growing up in Michigan, animals never far from her side. Moving about to varied US states, changing jobs, finding her husband, and overcoming medical and other challenges has honed Beth's story-telling and brought a unique worldview to her stories. Beth attempts now to complete her first novel, with some short stories here and there, but she will always continue to bring new worlds, strange characters and insightful situations to her readers.

Come back often to read new stories or, even better, sign up to the newsletter to be alerted to everything exciting!
